Welcome to the Centerstage Pixel Dropping Simulator!

The world's premiere centerstage pixel dropping simulator

Controls

R: Reset all pixels

SPACE: Hit/disturb the backdrop

W/A/S/D: Manipulate the left pixel

I/J/K/L: Manipulate the Right pixel

LEFT/RIGHT CLICK: Drop the corresponding pixel

LEFT/RIGHT ARROW: Cycles through colors for the corresponding pixel

Constraints

  • Both pixels must be dropped before a new cycle can take place.
  • The left and right pixels have a limited "closeness" due to real-world constraints of the claw thickness.
  • To simulate real-world cycles, pixel colors cannot be changed after dropping the first pixel. Decide what pixels to place before dropping them!
